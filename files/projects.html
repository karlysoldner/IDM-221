Science Projects

GreenStemNetwork.org holds a collection of projects I worked on during my co-op at the Philadelphia Water Department in the Office of Watersheds

The data section includes soil moisture monitoring data of a six-site arduino-based network of green stormwater infrastructure sites that I helped create and maintain. I also assisted with social media and web content. Check out the B^3 and SIM projects since I was active in those as well!

INSTITUTIONALIZING DELAY: FOUNDATION FUNDING AND THE CREATION OF U.S. CLIMATE CHANGE COUNTER-MOVEMENT ORGANIZATIONS

Published in Climactic Change in 2013, this paper investigates the funding of the climate change denial movement. I was a researcher on the project, searching public documents for linkages between climate deniers, their foundations, and their corporate interests.
